Dungeons & Crawling

I love all kind of dungeons:

  • old school dungeon crawler dungeons
  • MMO dungeons
  • Zelda dungeons
  • User made dungeons like in Neverwinter Nights
  • ...or even random generated roguelike dungeons

Nothing is more fun than explore the endless depths of a dungeon, fighting monsters and looting countless treasures. And the best thing? You can do it with a group of NPCs or even with other players in online games.
